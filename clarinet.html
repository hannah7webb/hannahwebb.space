projects clarinet writing

I've been playing the clarinet for over ten years, and it has deeply enriched my life. Right now, I study with Stephen Williamson, Principal Clarinet of the Chicago Symphony Orchestra, and Todd Levy, Principal Clarinet of the Milwaukee Symphony Orchestra, at the Bienen School of Music. Previously, I studied with Amanda Walker in Santa Monica, California for eight years.
