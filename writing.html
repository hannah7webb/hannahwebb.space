How to Win the Day

After my first year of college, I realized there’s no escaping pain, and that your real advantage comes from how well you’re conditioned to handle it. That got me thinking: what if you could earn points before the game even starts—proactively training your mind to handle whatever life throws your way—so you can start every day 1–0 against it.

The Modern Misinformation Crisis

Truth-seeking is foundational to human flourishing—so what happens when AI, instead of supporting that pursuit, capitalizes on human impulsivity? As the scientist uses it to cut corners, the journalist to craft seductive narratives, and the teenager to simulate connection, the line between reality and fabrication begins to blur.

What Music Taught Me About Life

For most of my childhood, I saw life as a jigsaw puzzle—something that could be solved cleanly, piece by piece. But through performing music, I discovered that life isn't meant to be solved, but experienced. Music taught me to embrace ambiguity, connect with others, and express what can't be said in words.
